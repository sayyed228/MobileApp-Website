TAGON FEATURES

EVENTS SHARING

Create events and send invites to your contacts. On acceptance of your invite, your contacts will be tagged along with the event.

COMMUTE COLLABORATION

Get regular pro active travel updataes on your contacts for your tagged events. Save on your phone bills. Get instant updates once the person reaches the event.

SECURITY AND PRIVACY

Tagon does not capture or reveal exact user location to ensure maximum security and privacy to the user. Tagon only provides real time user information based on high level user location details, traffic details and real time analysis.

PARK ASSIST

Easily locate your parked car using Tagon. No hardware needs to be installed in the car to locate it. Tagon does this through real time analytics.

TRAFFIC UPDATES

Tagon uses real time traffic based analytics to display user information and predict user start and expected arrival times

CHAT

Discuss on events, collaborate and finalize through our built in chat feature. Tagon provides event specific collaborative chat platform for instant messaging.
